FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

To become a member, you must be a called-to-bar legal practitioner registered with the Nigerian Bar Association. Visit our membership page to complete the registration form, submit required documents (Call to Bar Certificate, NYSC discharge certificate), and pay the prescribed dues.
The NBA Awka Bar Centre is located within the High Court Complex at the Anambra State Judiciary Headquarters in Awka. We are easily accessible from major landmarks in the state capital.
Members have access to our library at the Bar Centre during office hours. We also provide digital resources through our member portal. Contact the Publications Secretary for specific law reports or research materials.
Our Continuing Legal Education programs are held regularly throughout the year. Check our events calendar or contact the Events Committee at nbaawka@gmail.com for the schedule of upcoming programs and registration details.
