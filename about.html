About NBA Awka Branch

The Nigerian Bar Association, Awka Branch is the foremost professional body representing legal practitioners in Anambra State. Established in 1984, our branch has grown to become a beacon of legal excellence, professional integrity, and social responsibility.

We represent over 500 active members spanning diverse practice areas including corporate law, litigation, human rights, property law, and alternative dispute resolution. Our members serve as advocates, counselors, and champions of justice across the state and beyond.

Through our Bar Centre located at the High Court Complex in Awka, we provide a hub for professional development, networking, and access to legal resources. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of the legal profession while contributing meaningfully to the administration of justice in Nigeria.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To uphold the rule of law, promote the highest standards of legal practice and professional ethics, and protect the independence of the Bar; while fostering continuous legal education, unity, and welfare of members, and advancing access to justice and service to society within Awka and beyond..

  • Advance professional excellence through continuous legal education
  • Uphold the highest ethical standards in legal practice
  • Advocate for justice and human rights in our society
  • Support members in their professional development

Our Vision

To be a leading Bar Branch distinguished for excellence, integrity, and innovation in legal practice, committed to the advancement of justice, the rule of law, and the professional development and welfare of its members.

  • A branch where every member achieves professional excellence
  • A trusted voice in legal reform and policy advocacy
  • A center of excellence for legal education and research
  • A driving force for positive change in our community
